About Ayaan College of Nursing

Established in 1995 in Vill. Bhogiwal, Malerkotla, Punjab, Ayaan College of Nursing has built a legacy of excellence in professional nursing education.

Vision

To develop competent, compassionate and globally competitive healthcare professionals.

Mission

To provide academic excellence, ethical values and structured clinical training at Hicare Dugri.

Affiliations
